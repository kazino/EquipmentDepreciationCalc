Formula Reference

Age (months)= (Leaving Date - Purchase Date) / 365 * 12
Calculated Value= (Price / 36) * (36 - Age) - straight-line 36-month depreciation
Residual Value= Price * 10% - minimum floor value
Cost to Purchase= MAX(Calculated Value, Residual Value) - never below 10%
